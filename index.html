Speakers

Sandunes

Bombay-based producer Sanaya Ardeshir, aka Sandunes, combines the rapidly changing culture of Bombay and its surrounding areas to produce incredibly unique sound designs

Mitya

Hailing from the city of Kazan in Russia, Mitya Burmistrov aka MITYA is one of three artists recently commissioned by Red Bull to take part in their Searching for Sound series of documentaries.

Simón Mejía

With musical influences from Africa, Europe, the Caribbean and the indigenous population, the South American country of Colombia has long been fertile ground for new hybrid styles to develop.

Christopher Tignor

Christopher Tignor is an electronic musician, violinist, composer, and software engineer based in Brooklyn. Tignor currently holds two sponsorships: one from software developer Antares and one from German tuning fork manufacturer Wittner.

Bonobo

Simon Green, better known as Bonobo, has been releasing music for over 15 years. Over the course of six albums, the British producer has carried the torch for Ninja Tune’s aesthetic from the cut and paste, hip hop-inspired feels to largely acoustic and hypnotic textures.

Nick Hook

While Nick might be a new name for some people, his influence isn’t; having worked with Run the Jewels, Azealia Banks, Action Bronson, and the Depeche Mode producer Gareth Jones, to name just a few, he’s built up a reputation as something of a “producer’s producer”.
